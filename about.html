Who We Are

A trusted partner in global logistics with a focus on innovation and reliability

Pioneers of the Trans-Siberian Route

Founded in 2008, Trans-Siberian Shipping LLC began as a specialized freight forwarder focusing on the Trans-Siberian railway corridor. Our founders recognized the untapped potential of this route as the fastest land bridge between Asia and Europe.

Today, we've grown into a comprehensive global logistics provider while maintaining our core expertise in Trans-Siberian shipping. Our headquarters in Las Vegas, Nevada, serves as the hub for operations spanning three continents.

What sets us apart is our commitment to combining traditional logistics expertise with cutting-edge technology. We've invested heavily in digital tracking systems, automated warehouses, and AI-powered route optimization to deliver superior service.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our company history

2008

Company Founded

Established with focus on Trans-Siberian rail freight between China and Russia. Started with just 5 employees and 20 containers shipped monthly.

2012

European Expansion

Opened our first European office in Germany, extending our Trans-Siberian services to Western Europe.

2015

North American Headquarters

Moved our global headquarters to Las Vegas, Nevada, to better serve our growing North American clientele.

2018

Digital Transformation

Launched our proprietary tracking platform, providing real-time visibility for all shipments.

2021

Sustainability Initiative

Became the first Trans-Siberian logistics company to achieve carbon-neutral certification.

2023

Global Recognition

Awarded "Logistics Provider of the Year" by the International Shipping Federation.
