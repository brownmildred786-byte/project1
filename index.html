About Our Company

With over 15 years of experience in global logistics, we provide reliable and efficient shipping solutions worldwide.

Your Trusted Global Logistics Partner

Founded in 2008, Trans-Siberian Shipping LLC has grown from a regional freight forwarder to a global logistics powerhouse. Our headquarters in Las Vegas, Nevada, serves as the hub for our international operations spanning across three continents.

We specialize in the Trans-Siberian route, offering the fastest and most cost-effective land bridge between Asia and Europe, complemented by our extensive ocean and air freight services.

Our team of over 500 logistics professionals is dedicated to providing customized solutions tailored to your specific shipping needs, ensuring your cargo arrives on time and in perfect condition.
